Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 12, 2025

Hey there! Your privacy is super important to us at MyWall. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we keep it safe. We've tried to make it as simple as possible to understand.

What Information We Collect

  • Account Info: When you sign up, we ask for basic info like your name, email, and school. This helps us create and manage your account.
  • Profile Info: You can add more details to your profile, like a photo, a short bio, and your skills. This is totally optional, but it helps make your profile and resume look great.
  • Certificate Info: When you upload a certificate, we store the file and the details you provide, like the title, issuer, and date.
  • Usage Info: We collect some data on how you use MyWall, like which features you use most. This helps us make the app better for you.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Run the MyWall service and show you your certificates.
  • Let you create and download your resume.
  • Help you share your achievements when you choose to.
  • Improve the app and add new features you'll love.

Who Sees Your Information?

You are in control! Your profile and certificates are private by default.

  • We **never** sell your personal data to anyone. Period.
  • You can choose to share a specific certificate or a collection. Only people with the special link you share can see it.
  • If you apply for a job or to a school through a future MyWall feature, we will only share the information you approve beforehand.

Your Choices & Controls

You have the right to:

  • View and edit your profile information at any time on your 'My Profile' page.
  • Delete any certificate you've uploaded.
  • Download your data or delete your account from the 'My Account' page.

Keeping Your Data Safe

We take the security of your data seriously. We use strong security measures like encryption and best-practice server configurations to protect your data from being lost or accessed without permission.

Changes to This Policy

We might update this policy sometimes. If we make big changes, we'll let you know by email or a notice in the app before the changes take effect.

Questions?

If you have any questions about this policy, just drop us an email at privacy@mywall.com, and we'll be happy to help!
